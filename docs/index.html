Home

Maker: Online is a multiplayer gaming experience by Basick Games. Join in the fun and connect to servers hosted by other players, or, write up your own gamemode and host it on a server for other players to join.

The aim of Maker: Online is to abstract away many of the complexities around building a multiplayer game, and rapidly speed up the development process so that players can create whatever they desire with ease. To achieve this, Maker: Online focuses on providing the core functionality, so that creators can focus on implementing features for their gamemode.

Feature-driven Gamemodes

Maker: Online strives to provide all the core functionality so that the creators can focus on what matters most, the features. As such, the look and feel of each gamemode will remain largely the same, but the story and gameplay will be totally tailored by the gamemode creator.

For instance: scenery & character models, audio, physics, networking, ingame chat, and much more are all built into the core functionality that comprises Maker: Online. The story, gameplay mechanics, spawns, stats, and much more are changeable and totally unique to each gamemode.

Inspiration

Maker: Online and its design philosophy has been primarily influenced by SA:MP (San Andreas Multiplayer), a multiplayer mod for the popular action-adventure game by Rockstar Games. Every server looked and felt largely the same, but each one was completely different and unique in its own right due to the immense creativity of the SA:MP community.

By embracing that same philosophy and applying it in Maker: Online - a game built specifically for creating gamemodes - Basick Games hopes to empower the players and enable them to create multiplayer games that before they merely wished for.
